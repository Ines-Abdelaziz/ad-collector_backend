AdAnalyst Survey Panel

Help researchers understand how to make Facebook ads safer and more fair.

  Sign Up Now

  • Install an advertising transparency tool that will anonymously and safely collect the advertisements that appear on your Facebook Feed.
  • Take a 15 minute survey at the end of each month.
  • Earn up to $400 for full participation in the year-long study.
  • Find out: Who is targeting you with Ads on Facebook, what Facebook knows about you, and why you're receiving the ads you see on Facebook.


How does it work?

What You Will Do:

We will ask you to install AdAnalyst on your browser. AdAnalyst is a tool that we have built to anonymously collect the advertisements that you see on Facebook. We will also ask you to complete a 15-minute survey at the end of every month for 12 months (you may chose to skip a survey but continue with the study).
This study has been reviewed and approved by the Ethics Board of the University of Saarland on behalf of the Max Planck Institute

Payment:


You will get paid $50 for installing AdAnalyst and completing the first survey.
You will be paid $25 for completing each monthly survey.
You will be paid a $50 bonus for finishing all 12 months of the study.
You can earn up to $400 for completing the study.
All payments will be Amazon.com giftcards that will be sent to your email address.

What data we collect from you:

  • The ads you receive while you browse Facebook
  • The accompanied explanations that Facebook provides
  • Periodically, your Ads Preferences page
  • Your answers to our survey questions.
  • Hashed versions of your user id.
    Hashing prevents us from knowing your actual user id. We will not store your actual user id.
  • Your email address. We will use your email address to distribute payments. We will not store your email address in connection to your data, nor will we ever release your email address to anyone else. We will delete your email address immediately after the conclusion of the study.


What data we DO NOT collect from you:

  • Your messages
  • Your posts
  • Your likes
  • Anything else that we do not explicitly state


How is my data going to be used?

Your data is going to be used in our research, we will only collect anonymous data. We will never pair your data with your identity (email, etc.) nor will we release any information about your identity.



Is my data going to be safe?

We will store your data in the servers of the Max Planck Institute for Software Systems (MPI-SWS), protected by the MPI-SWS firewall.
